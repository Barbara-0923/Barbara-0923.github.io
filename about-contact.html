© 2023 Barbara Wang Photography
Photographer / Creative Director / Cinematographer currently based in LA

Lauren takes a unique approach to her work – rooted with nostalgic and moody undertones, her imagery tells authentic stories about femininity, diversity and embracing emotion. Using creative set design and cinematic motifs, she forges a dream landscape for her subject matter. Lauren is a visual creator with a strong base on expressive imagination in photography & motion.

For 10+ years, Lauren has been honored to work with names big and small to construct visual narratives and curate strong brand identities.
